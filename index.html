Welcome

Authentic Ethiopian Fashion

Welcome to HABESHA TRADITIONAL CLOTHE, where we celebrate the rich heritage of Ethiopian traditional fashion. Our collection features handwoven Habesha Kemis, traditional shoes (Kuru), Netelas, and authentic accessories.

Every piece tells a story of Ethiopian craftsmanship passed down through generations.
