# PITCH: HABESHA TRADITIONAL CLOTHE - Ethiopian Traditional Boutique Website

## "Preserving Ethiopian Heritage Through Fashion"

---

### THE PROBLEM

Ethiopian traditional fashion is rich with history, but authentic Habesha clothing and traditional footwear are difficult to find online. Many Ethiopians abroad and those seeking authentic cultural wear struggle to find quality traditional garments. Meanwhile, local artisans struggle to reach customers beyond their communities.

---

### THE SOLUTION

HABESHA TRADITIONAL CLOTHE is an online boutique that bridges this gap—connecting Ethiopian artisans with customers who value authentic traditional fashion. Our website makes Ethiopian heritage accessible to everyone, 24/7.

### PAYMENT METHODS (ETHIOPIAN CONTEXT)

We understand Ethiopian customers prefer local payment options:

- **Telebirr** — Mobile money payments via +251 91 234 5678
- **Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE)** — Direct bank transfers
- **Awash Bank** — Alternative bank transfer option
- **Cash on Delivery** — Pay when you receive your order (Addis Ababa)
