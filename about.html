Our Story

Preserving Ethiopian heritage through authentic fashion

EST. 2020

Preserving Ethiopian Heritage

ባህላዊ ለምንግር was born from a passion to preserve and promote authentic Ethiopian traditional fashion. Founded in 2020 by Tinsae Gebre, our boutique connects Ethiopian artisans with fashion lovers worldwide.

We work directly with skilled craftspeople across Ethiopia - from Addis Ababa to Gondar, from Harar to Jimma. Each piece in our collection represents hours of meticulous handwork and centuries of tradition.

THE FOUNDER

Meet Tinsae

"I believe that traditional Ethiopian fashion is not just clothing - it's a celebration of our identity, our history, and our people's artistry."

Tinsae trained as a fashion designer in Addis Ababa before starting this venture to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern fashion needs.
